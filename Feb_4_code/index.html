Sponge Cake

This is a very simple dish loved by very simple people and made by very simple people with simple HTML and CSS

Ingredients

  • Milk - 2 tbsp
  • Coco Powder - 2 tsp
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Baking Powder - 2 tsp
  • Egg - 2
  • Oil - as required
  • Vanilla Essence - 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Be nice to the baking pot and glaze it with some oil
  2. In a bowl mix 2 eggs with 2 cups of flour and 1 cup of sugar
  3. Add baking powder and some milk then mix till thickens
  4. Pre heat oven at 350°F
  5. 2 < 3
  6. Pour the mix in baking pot and bake for 45 mins
  7. Cool and serve
